SURREAL SKY

SURREAL_SKY is an AI-driven digital art series that explores the intersection of memory, technology, and the hyper-stylized aesthetic of a futuristic dreamscape. Rooted in my experiences living in Japan right out of university, this body of work blends nostalgia with speculative fiction, creating an alternative reality shaped by the fluid boundaries between human creativity and machine intelligence.

AI-generated · text-to-image · Photoshop image compositing

The series is defined by a curated balance of glitch aesthetics, cyberpunk influences, and soft dreamlike hues, layered compositions integrating elements of surrealism, anime-inspired cyber aesthetics, and traditional art motifs. Each piece is refined through iterative prompt engineering and manual post-processing, pushing high-resolution upscaling techniques to extract and enhance fine detail from every frame.

ComfyUI · Topaz Upscaling · 2024-2025

PROCESS

I started off with a very simple Midjourney image, which was a combination of various style references. Eventually, I got tired of the Midjourney look and brought everything into ComfyUI, where I trained my own LoRAs to create better images with FLUX.

I use this base image as the building block. Back in Comfy, I begin to upscale it, refine it, recompose it, Photoshop it, upscale it again, and color correct it. My background as an art director, photo editor, and designer has definitely come in handy when enhancing AI imagery.
