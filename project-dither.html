DITHER

A collection of generative sound machines rendered in 1-bit dithered black and white. Each piece is a self-contained procedural world — organic forms, attractors, deep ocean, cosmic phenomena — paired with ambient sound design. Every machine runs as a 20-second perfect loop with audio built from synthesized drones, noise, and environmental textures.
