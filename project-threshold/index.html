The 72-Hour Promise

Research shows 72 hours breaks digital dependency. We've found it takes slightly longer to hear The Quiet — four to five days. By day seven, the halls feel different. The light changes. Spaces that seemed identical begin to reveal their character.

By month three, Wanderers stop counting days entirely.
