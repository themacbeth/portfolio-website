MAX_MACBETH_

A series of synthetic portraits — faces that don't belong to anyone, built from fragments of memory, noise, and iterative generation.

Each character exists in a single frame. No backstory. No context. Just a face staring back with something that almost reads as intention.

Stable Diffusion · Custom LoRAs · 2024

The uncanny valley was always the destination, not the obstacle. I wanted faces that land in that gap — too real to dismiss, too wrong to trust.

Everyone in this series is nobody. That's the point.

PROCESS

Started with base models and trained character-specific LoRAs to maintain consistency across expression shifts. Lighting was controlled through ControlNet depth maps — each portrait lit as if it were a single-source studio shot, pushing shadow hard to one side.

Post-processing in Photoshop to deepen contrast and pull back saturation. The final color grade sits somewhere between fashion editorial and surveillance footage.
