HYPER_

HYPER_ is a collection of looping, AI-driven animations that embrace abstraction at full velocity. Bathed in high-saturation neons and warped light, each piece feels like a glitching pulse — fluid, untethered, and emotionally charged.

There's no narrative, no characters — just the pure sensation of movement and light, evoking a hyper-anime aesthetic pushed into the uncanny. These loops inhabit a space where form breaks down and the surreal logic of machine vision takes over.

ComfyUI · AnimateDiff · After Effects · 2023

Just the pure sensation of movement and light.

PROCESS

Each animation was generated through a text-to-video pipeline using ComfyUI and AnimateDiff, with heavily anime-trained checkpoints and LoRAs guiding the aesthetic. Prompts were designed to provoke strangeness, intensity, and fluidity — embracing the hallucinatory tendencies of the models.

After generation, every piece was fine-tuned in After Effects to enhance glow, contrast, and polish. The results are seamlessly looping fragments of synthetic motion, selected through a rigorous, iterative curation process that prioritizes rhythm, silhouette, and visual impact.
