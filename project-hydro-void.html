HYDRO_VOID

liminal aquatic environments

seasick

A series of AI-generated video loops that plunge viewers into submerged, impossible spaces—subterranean caverns, flooded voids, and bottomless shafts of water and silence. The work draws from the visual grammar of FPV drone footage, urban exploration videos, and first-person documentation of extreme physical spaces: crane climbs, lightning towers, and deep underwater caves.

By blending those adrenaline-fueled perspectives with a personal fear of the open ocean, the series creates a paradoxical tension—both exhilarating and meditative, vast and claustrophobic. The black circle, reappears here as a silent, gravitational pull. What begins as motion becomes hypnosis; what feels real collapses into dream logic and digital artifact.
